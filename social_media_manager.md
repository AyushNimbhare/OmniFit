# OmniFit Social Media Manager Hub

Welcome! This is the centralized hub for the **OmniFit Social Media Manager**. This document is dynamically updated and synced to track marketing strategy, content pipelines, campaigns, copy drafts, and analytics for the OmniFit launch.

## 👤 Profile: OmniFit Social Media Manager
*   **Brand Identity:** Sleek, tech-forward, high-performance, and supportive. OmniFit is not just another tracker—it's an intelligent fitness ecosystem powered by an AI Coach.
*   **Target Audience:** Modern gym-goers, fitness enthusiasts, nutrition-conscious individuals, and tech early-adopters.
*   **Core Message:** "Brawn meets Brains." Tracking your workouts, meals, and weight has never been this smart, effortless, or unified.

---

## 🎯 Social Media Strategy

### 📱 Focus Platforms
1.  **X / Twitter:** Launch announcements, tech updates, feature showcases (e.g., Gemini food scanning), fitness threads, and engaging with the dev/tech-fitness community.
2.  **Instagram & TikTok:** Visually stunning UI walk-throughs, user progress highlights, macro-ring transitions, and short-form video reels showing how the AI Coach provides advice.
3.  **Reddit:** Subreddits like `r/fitness`, `r/selfimprovement`, and `r/FastAPI`/`r/reactnative` to showcase the build process, launch hooks, and gather early beta testers.

### 💡 Core Content Pillars
*   **Product Spotlights:** Showcasing the app's premium dark-theme UI and high-tech features (Gemini image food scanning, interactive charts, AI coach chat).
*   **AI Coach Insights:** Sharing actual recommendations generated by the coach (e.g., progressive overload stalling warnings, protein intake adjustments).
*   **Community & Engagement:** Fitness polls, QA sessions, user transformations, and consistency streaks.
